About us

  2. About us

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is centered on the principles of decentralization, security, and innovation. We believe that blockchain technology and token engineering hold the key to creating a more equitable and accessible financial system that can benefit marginalized communities in Africa and beyond.

Our Mission

The mission of the Thelpis ecosystem is to promote financial inclusion for marginalized communities in Africa by creating a decentralized and secure financial system using blockchain technology and digital tokens.

Our Vission

We envision a future where all individuals, regardless of their location or financial status, have equal access to financial services and the resources they need to thrive.

Key Of Success

Our commitment to using innovative technologies like blockchain and token engineering, our understanding of the unique challenges faced by marginalized communities in Africa, and our partnerships with local organizations and communities.

Why Us

Commitment to Innovation

We are passionate about creating a more inclusive and equitable future for all, and we believe that our innovative solution has the potential to make a real difference in the lives of marginalized communities in Africa.

We are committed to using technology for good and working towards a future where everyone has equal access to financial services and the resources they need to thrive.

Blockchain and Financial Education

Live Learning Facility

The Thelpis Blockchain Education Facility is a comprehensive learning center that provides individuals with the knowledge and skills to understand the revolutionary potential of blockchain technology. Our focus is on educating people about the financial aspects of blockchain, including digital currencies, decentralized finance (DeFi), and the potential for increased financial inclusion for marginalized communities.

Through interactive courses, hands-on workshops, and expert-led sessions, participants will gain a thorough understanding of the underlying principles and practical applications of blockchain technology in finance. Whether you are an individual seeking to increase your financial literacy or a business looking to explore new opportunities in the blockchain space, the Thelpis Blockchain Education Facility is the perfect place to start..
