Using Blockchain Technology, AI, & Digital Tokens

Thelpis can create a decentralized and secure financial system that provides a more inclusive and accessible platform for financial transactions. This is a significant innovation in financial inclusion, as traditional financial systems can often be expensive and difficult to access for marginalized communities.

Thelpis Platform Scalability

Thelpis is scalable in its approach to promoting financial inclusion. The Thelpis protocol is designed to be used by individuals and businesses in marginalized communities across Africa, and it has the potential to reach a large number of people even those who are not marginalized.


By using blockchain technology and digital tokens, Thelpis can create a financial system that is easily accessible and can be used by anyone with a smartphone or internet connection.
